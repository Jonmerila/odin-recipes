Description

The crockpot buffalo chiecken is great for hearty sandwiches that will please those who love Buffalo chicken wings.

Ingridients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (5.2 dl or 17.5 fl ounce) bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • 1/2 (30 g or 1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls ( or the bread buns of your choice), split lengthwise

Directions

  1. Place chicken breasts into the slow cooker; pour in 3/4 of the wing saunce and ranch dressing mix.
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.
  3. Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks. Stir in the butter.
  4. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaining Buffalo sauce.
