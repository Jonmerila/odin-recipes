Description

In this page, we will be going through the worlds best dish in my opinion, the Lasagna.

Ingridients for making a Lasagna

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products (Crushed tomatoes, canned tomatoes, tomato paste).
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings (Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper).
  • Lasagna noodles/plates
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

How to make the lasagna step-by-step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to layer Lasagna step-by-step

The detailed layering instructions step-by-step are shows below:

  1. Meat sauce.
  2. Noodles.
  3. Ricotta mixture.
  4. Mozzarella slices.
  5. Meat sauce.
  6. Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with remaining parmesan.
